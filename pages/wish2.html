... AAJA YAAR JALDI SE ...



Teri ye saaree badi kamaal ki lagti hai ,
jab se dekha tujhako meri bhi halat thodi kharab si lagti hai

Karo es nachij pr bhi thodi dya ,
aap to surat se hi badi pyari si lagti han .


Kab aaoge milne hamse , kab hame bhi darsh dikhaoge ,
ham bhi baithe han entezaar me aapke , kab aake hame bhi bulaoge.
teri ek aawaj par yun bhage chale aayenge ,
tum aawaj to dena ham khuda ke paas se bhi mohlat maang aayenge .
entezaar tera es kadar hai es dil ko ,
soch ke baithe han abhi se , tere sath ke har ek pal ko.
kab tum aaoge , kab ham bhi mil paayenge ,
dhyan rakhana kahin jayda der na ho jaye , khuda ko bhi kab tk rok payenge
karenge entezar tera aakhari sans tk ,
par tum thodi jaldi krna aakhari sans bhi kab tk rok paayenge....
